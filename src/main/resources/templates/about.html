About UEP Lost & Found Hub

Connecting lost items with their owners since 2024. Our mission is to build a community where helping each other becomes second nature.

Back to Home

Our Mission

To create a seamless platform where the UEP community can report lost items, return found belongings, and celebrate every successful reunion. We believe in the power of community and technology working together for good.

Search & Report

Easily search for lost items or report found ones with detailed descriptions and categories.

Notifications

Get instant notifications when items matching your description are reported.
