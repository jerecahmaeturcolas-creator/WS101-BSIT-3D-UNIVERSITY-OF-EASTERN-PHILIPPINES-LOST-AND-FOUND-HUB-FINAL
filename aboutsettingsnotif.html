About UEP Lost & Found

Our Mission

To provide a secure, efficient, and user-friendly platform that reunites lost items with their owners while fostering a culture of honesty and community support within the UEP campus.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and effective lost and found service in Philippine universities, setting the standard for campus community support systems and technological innovation.

Our Story

The UEP Lost & Found System was born out of a simple observation in 2022: countless valuable items were being lost on campus with no efficient way to return them to their owners. What started as a student-led initiative has grown into the official university-wide platform for item recovery.

Today, we serve over 5,000 students, faculty, and staff members, leveraging technology to create a community where lost items find their way home. Our platform combines artificial intelligence with human verification to ensure maximum recovery rates while maintaining privacy and security.

Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, student/faculty ID, email address, contact number
  • Lost Item Information: Descriptions, photos, locations where items were lost
  • Found Item Information: Descriptions, photos, locations where items were found
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our system, login times, features used

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To match lost items with found items
  • To notify you when your lost items are found
  • To verify your identity when claiming items
  • To improve our system and services
  • To send important updates and notifications

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary:

  • Active account data: Until account deletion
  • Lost/found item records: 1 year after resolution
  • System logs: 6 months
  • Analytics data: 2 years (anonymized)

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request data deletion
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of data collection
